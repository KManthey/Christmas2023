Happy Holidays From the Mantheys

Happy holidays dear friends and family! May the new year be filled with joy, love, and friendship.

We had a busy 2023. I went from contract to hire with my company and Dale changed positions with his company. We finally got our pool enclosure replaced after it collapsed in a hurricane last year (with a new pool deck too). Dale and I visited San Diego just before Christmas last year then we celebrated my birthday in Rome (in Feb.). We recently visited Savannah and Ashville to see the fall colors.

Marley, Bob, and Kiki also send their love too. They are doing well although Marley is showing signs of aging (as are we). We were heartbroken to have to remove our giant oak tree this year as it was causing a crack to form in the garage floor. Please stay safe and stay in touch.

Love, Kathy, Dale, Bob, Marley, and Kiki
